Insider threat
Most elusive threats to an organization comes
from within!
Causes:
•
Malicious intent (revenge)
•
By mistake
•
Negligence

--- Page 4 ---
INSIDER THREAT
Insider threat manifestation:
• Malicious insiders: who intentionally harm the company.
• Negligent insiders: who inadvertently cause damage through
carelessness.
• Accidental insiders: who fall victims to phishing or social
engineering attacks.

--- Page 5 ---
Preventing Insider threats
How to prevent insider threats?
• Technical controls such as access management and user
behavior analytics.
• Organizational measures like regular training and a culture of
security awareness:
• Encouraging employees to report suspicious activities.
• Ensuring they understand the protocols for handling sensitive
information.

Ransomware
What is Ransomware?
• Malicious software that
encrypts files on a device or
network, holding them
hostage until a ransom is
paid. Usually in some form
of cryptocurrency.

How Ransomware Spreads?
• Ransomware infiltrates into systems in the same way as other
malicious codes do.
• For example, using phishing emails, exploiting vulnerabilities, or
malicious downloads.

--- Page 9 ---
How to protect against ransomware?
• Foundational Cybersecurity Measures
• Regular software updates. (To patch vulnerabilities)
• Robust endpoint security solutions. (Using robust antivirus and anti-
malware tools)
• Educating employees about phishing. (Training employees to recognize
phishing attempts and other threats)

--- Page 10 ---
How to protect against ransomware?
• Specific Ransomware Countermeasures
• Immutable storage architectures.
• Application whitelisting:
• Restricting administrative
• Network segmentation

--- Page 11 ---
Advanced defense tactics
• End-to-end encryption
• Incident response
• Threat hunting activities
• Threat intelligence services
This is an application of the Defense in Depth strategy.
Combining general malware defenses with ransomware-
specific tactics offers the best protection. It's not just about
resisting attacks but also recovering quickly when they
occur.
